Before the fame

Before he became famous with his hit single, "There's no bathroom in my house," he was very lame and uncool. His eyesight was so poor even though he ate so many carrots his hair turned orange. But he did have high aspirations and a cool friend named Joel.

The Glory Days

After a quick rise to the top, he enjoyed several years of prosperity and riches. He went on talk shows and kissed babies. He had a brief stint as clownfish in the animated short "The Little Mermaid." However, he soon got addicted to drugs and alcohol and spent several years in either rehabilitation or jail from illegal activities related to backdoor bounce house dealing and hand sanitizer.
